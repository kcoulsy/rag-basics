Customer: Our webhook endpoint keeps timing out.
Agent: Webhooks must respond with 2xx within 10 seconds or we retry with exponential backoff.
Customer: Our handler does heavy processing on each event.
Agent: Acknowledge immediately with 200 and process asynchronously in a background job.
Customer: How many retries do you attempt?
Agent: Up to 8 retries over 24 hours. After that, failed events appear in the webhook dead-letter log.
Customer: Can we replay failed events?
Agent: Yes. Open Developers, Webhooks, then Failed Deliveries, and replay individual events or batches.
