Customer: Can I pay annually instead of monthly?
Agent: Yes. Annual billing saves 20% compared to paying month to month.
Customer: If I switch mid-cycle, what happens?
Agent: We credit unused monthly time toward the annual plan and charge the prorated difference.
Customer: When does the annual period start?
Agent: It starts immediately upon switch. Your renewal date moves to one year from the conversion date.
Customer: Can I go back to monthly later?
Agent: You can switch at renewal. Mid-cycle switches from annual to monthly aren't supported.
