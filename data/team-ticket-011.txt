Customer: How do I add teammates to my workspace?
Agent: Go to Workspace Settings, then Members, and click Invite. Enter their email and choose a role.
Customer: What roles are available?
Agent: Owner, Admin, Editor, and Viewer. Only Owners can manage billing and delete the workspace.
Customer: Is there a per-seat charge?
Agent: Team workspaces require a Pro or Enterprise plan. Pro includes 5 seats; additional seats are billed monthly.
Customer: Can editors delete projects?
Agent: Editors can create and edit projects but cannot delete the workspace or manage billing.
