Customer: Does Premium include phone support?
Agent: Premium includes priority email support with a 24-hour SLA. Phone support is Enterprise only.
Customer: What's the difference between priority email and standard?
Agent: Priority tickets are routed to a senior queue and typically answered within 4 to 8 hours on business days.
Customer: Is weekend support included?
Agent: Premium covers weekend email support for severity-1 outages. General questions are answered Monday through Friday.
Customer: How do I mark a ticket urgent?
Agent: Select "Production outage" when submitting. Misuse of urgent flags may limit priority access.
