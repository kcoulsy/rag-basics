Customer: Please delete my account under the right to be forgotten.
Agent: I can start that process. Deletion is permanent after a 14-day grace period.
Customer: Why is there a grace period?
Agent: It prevents accidental deletion and allows you to cancel the request by logging in within 14 days.
Customer: What about backups?
Agent: Backups are purged within 30 days per our retention policy. Legal holds may extend retention if required.
Customer: Will you notify third parties?
Agent: We notify integrated services to delete linked data where we act as data controller.
