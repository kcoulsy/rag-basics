Customer: I'd like to cancel my subscription.
Agent: I can help with that. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
Customer: Will I receive a refund?
Agent: Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once a billing cycle has started.
Customer: What happens to my data?
Agent: We retain account data for 90 days after cancellation. After that it is permanently deleted.
Customer: Can I reactivate before then?
Agent: Yes, you can reactivate your account during the 90-day retention period.
