Customer: Do you ship to PO boxes?
Agent: Standard shipping supports PO boxes via USPS. Express and overnight carriers require a street address.
Customer: I only have a PO box on a military base.
Agent: We ship to APO, FPO, and DPO addresses using USPS. Delivery times are typically 2 to 4 weeks.
Customer: Are there item restrictions?
Agent: Lithium batteries and certain electronics can't ship to military addresses. Your cart item, a paperback book, is allowed.
Customer: Is tracking available?
Agent: Tracking is available once the package leaves our warehouse and enters the military mail system.
