Customer: How do I change my password without using forgot password?
Agent: Log in, go to Settings, then Security, and select Change Password.
Customer: Do I need my current password?
Agent: Yes. For security, you must enter your current password before setting a new one.
Customer: What are the password requirements?
Agent: At least 12 characters with one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character.
Customer: Will changing my password log out other devices?
Agent: Yes. All active sessions are invalidated. You'll need to sign in again on each device.
