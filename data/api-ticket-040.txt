Customer: Is there a sandbox API for testing?
Agent: Yes. Toggle sandbox mode in Developers to get separate keys and a test data environment.
Customer: Does sandbox data affect production?
Agent: No. Sandbox and production are fully isolated. Keys cannot cross environments.
Customer: Are rate limits the same?
Agent: Sandbox limits are lower: 50 requests per minute to prevent abuse during development.
Customer: Can we reset sandbox data?
Agent: Yes. Use Reset Sandbox in developer settings. This deletes all test projects and cannot be undone.
