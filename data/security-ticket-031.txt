Customer: Is my account protected if I reuse a password from another site?
Agent: We hash passwords and never store them in plain text, but reused passwords are risky if another site is breached.
Customer: Should I change it?
Agent: Yes. Use a unique password and enable two-factor authentication for stronger protection.
Customer: Do you support passkeys?
Agent: Yes. Passkeys are available on web and mobile under Settings, Security, then Passkeys.
Customer: If I add a passkey, can I still use my password?
Agent: Yes. Passkeys are an additional sign-in method. Your password remains active unless you disable it.
