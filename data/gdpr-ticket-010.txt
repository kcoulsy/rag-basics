Customer: I need a copy of all personal data you hold about me.
Agent: You can request a data export under GDPR from Settings, then Privacy, then Download My Data.
Customer: How long does that take?
Agent: Exports are usually ready within 48 hours. You'll receive an email with a secure download link valid for 7 days.
Customer: Can I request deletion at the same time?
Agent: Yes, but deletion is irreversible. We recommend downloading your export first.
Customer: What data is included?
Agent: Profile information, billing history, project metadata, audit logs, and support ticket history tied to your account.
